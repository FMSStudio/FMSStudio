Taught by an established music professional

Have you longed to play a musical instrument, but for one reason or another never got around to it? However, you still desire to enjoy the wonderful feeling of knowing how to play an instrument.

Why wait? My name is Dan Knudsen and I have been playing guitar, as well as other instruments, since 1962. I am a retired music therapist and now teach various stringed instruments at my home studio.

Here's what I can teach you how to play

I teach most stringed instruments (except violin, viola, & cello). I also teach music theory teaching both standard notation as well as tablature.

  • Guitar & Bass
  • Beginning Banjo
  • Autoharp & Dulcimer
  • Mandolin
  • Beginning Rock and Blues
  • Praise and Worship

I have an effective teaching style that takes you where you are and builds you up slowly giving you confidence and joy as you become a better musician.
